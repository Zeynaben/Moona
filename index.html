About Moona

Welcome to MOONA! - your cozy corner for the 2 KPOP groups
- ENHYPEN & TXT.
This fan project was made to bring the world a little closer to ours, with a fun mix of visuals, music and cute surprises along the way.
Here you will find member pages, updates and media from TXT & Enhypen, and some photocard surprises!
Made by a fan, for fans. Because let’s be real... our love for them deserves its own digital home.
